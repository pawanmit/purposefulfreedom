About Me

Pawan Mittal

Living in Present. Dreaming in Eternity.

Hi, I am Pawan. I work as a software developer for a fortune 500 company in San Francisco. At one point I would have been proud of this introduction. Not so much anymore. There are many reasons for this, the biggest being that I no longer let circumstances like a job or relationship define my identity. A job limits the number of ways I can put myself out there and add value. My job values my analytical skills and pays handsomely for that. But I yearn to create without having to worry about bottomline and performance reviews. This website is my attempt to do so.

I believe that all of us have the potential to transforms our present dreams into reality so we can dream new dreams. But we are taught to play safe and not dream too big least we get hurt. This website is my attempt to connect to other dreamers out there so one day no dream is considered too big or too wild.

Please don't hesitate to drop me a line if you have any questions about SFM or if you would just like to connect as a friend.

My SFM Journey

My metaphorical journey to SFM started with a real one across continental United States. After having worked in corporate world for many years I did not want to be a part of the rat race. I wanted to be free from the drudgery of nine-to-five. So in May 2017 I decided to spend the summer on a farm with my family. I drove from California to Maryland and spent one month on the farm. On the way back from Maryland to California I drove through many states and saw sites that were awe inspiring and defies logic.

The journey had transformed me in more ways that I can describe. I had tasted pure, unadulterated freedom. For three months, I was on the road in a minivan state-hopping. I dined in New Jersey at two in the morning, went to a wild life safari in Hamilton, Canada on a Monday afternoon, and spent a Friday casino hopping in Vegas. I loved that life and couldn't get enough of it.

But there was one ingredient missing from my trip - purpose. Just having fun was not fulfilling enough. I wanted to express myself through my God given gifts and create something of value.

After coming back as I was looking for opportunities that would allow the freedom I had experienced with the purpose I was seeking, I came across Six Figure Mentors.

After being with SFM for 6 months I can confidently say that my prayers have been answered. SFM aligned my value of freedom and purpose perfectly. Along with the opportunity to create financial freedom, it has provided a platform where I am able to channel my creativity towards adding value in my life and the world around me.

For any questions about myself and my experience with SFM please do not hesitate to shoot me an email or book an appointment with me.
